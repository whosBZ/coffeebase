# Application Overview and Ideas

## What is Coffeebase 

* A place for coffee enthusiast and lovers of coffee to share information

## Why was this created

* When I moved to Dublin there was no single location for finding good coffee bean sellers
* Reddit worked but it was not a create single location for all information and also was specific to locations
* This application should assist in catalogging beans and cafes so when people travel or move they can easily find places
* It will also be a single location for people to share thoughts, ideas, recipes for coffee

## What should the application allow 

* People can upload names and locations of cafes and provide information/photos/reviews of them
* People can upload various beans with information on where to find them and if they are worth it
* People can upload create profiles for themselves describing their experiences with coffee and become experts on the page
* People can upload recipes for various coffee equipment for home coffee making
