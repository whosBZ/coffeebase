
# Coffeebase

A central location for coffee enthusiasts around the world to find good coffee close to them. From beans to cafes, this will be the location 




## Roadmap

- Cafe Directory to store, search and review cafes in your area

- Bean Directory to upload beans with reviews as well as linking them to cafes or locations you have bought them

- Equipment Directory for building a database of all used coffee equipment from Espresso Machines to Pourovers. 

- Recipe directory that will allow for linking beans to equipment and what your setup was when making your coffee
